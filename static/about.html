About FlightGrab

FlightGrab helps you find cheap flights from major US airports. We aggregate prices and show you the best deals.

Booking disclaimer: FlightGrab does not sell flights. When you click "Book Now," we redirect you to airline websites or Google Flights to complete your purchase. Your booking is made directly with the airline or booking site—we are a search tool, not a travel agency.

How It Works

  • We collect flight prices from public sources daily.
  • You browse deals by date, origin, and destination.
  • When you're ready to book, we send you to the airline or a booking site.

Features

  • Cheapest flights across many destinations
  • Flexible date search and price calendar
  • Price alerts (get notified when prices drop)
  • Saved routes for quick access
