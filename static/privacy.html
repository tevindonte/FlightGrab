Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

FlightGrab ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign up, we store your email and name on our servers.
  • Saved preferences: Routes you save, price alerts you set, and preferred date ranges you store.
  • Usage data: We may log anonymized requests to improve the service.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide the service: showing flight deals, sending price alerts, and remembering your preferences.

Booking and Third-Party Sites

Important: When you click "Book Now" or similar buttons, we redirect you to airline or travel booking sites. Those sites have their own privacy policies. We do not control how third parties handle your data once you leave FlightGrab.

Cookies

We use cookies for authentication and session management. Third-party services (e.g., our auth provider) may set additional cookies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the site (e.g., database hosting, email delivery for alerts).

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at the email on our About page.
